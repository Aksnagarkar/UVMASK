We are introducing UV MASK for your hygeine

Using the N-95 mask and the surgical mask is creating huge waste. Which leads to pollution which is harmful to animals.

Solution is to design a mask which gets sanitize by itself. As it is not possible to wash the mask every now and then so, we have come up with the idea of UV SANITIZATION MASK which will sanitize the mask with the help of UV-C LED and filtration system.

UV SANITIZATION MASK WITH RESPIRATORY MONITORING SYSTEM

Based on high intensity UV-C LED's

Due to COVID-19, hygiene is a major concern for everyone, and wearing the mask is as essential as breathing. So, the mask must be clean and germ-free. As in many cases, it becomes difficult to keep it clean and we can’t wash it if it comes in contact with any surface which is not safe as far as hygiene is concerned. Many people are using the surgical mask and they throw them after the use it results in waste and animals face trouble.

Whenever the mask comes in contact with any virus, bacteria it will get killed and the mask will get sanitize and we won’t have to buy a new mask and it will reduce pollution.
